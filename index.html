My Neighborhood

I live in Hampden, located in Northern Baltimore. There's a famous street called The Avenue (West 36th Street), known for it's trendy shops and diners. I also live nearby 34th street, which hosts "Miracle on the 34th Street"every year, where you can see the houses all covered in extensive Christmas lights. The Charmery, a popular ice cream place, is also in the neighborhood. Every spring in Hampden is HONfest and in fall, Hampdenfest, which celebrate Baltimore's art and food.


My School

The elementary and middle school that I attended for the past eight years is called Baltimore Montessori Public Charter School. It's a public school that is very different from most. The school uses the methods developed by Maria Montessori, an Italian doctor and teacher, unusual for a woman in the early 1900's. Her educational methods focus on children learning naturally, with much emphasis on community and freedom with responsibilty. It does not believe in homework or uniforms.
